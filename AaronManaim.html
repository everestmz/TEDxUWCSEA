Speaker Profile

Aaron Maniam

Having graduated at top of his class at Yale and Oxford. Aaron now works in the Singapore Government with the Ministry of Trade and Industry after working as a diplomat in Washington and as political strategist at various think tanks. He is an accomplished debater, poet and philosopher, his primary interests is the evolving nature of our political leaders. His talk, inspired by how the modern generation “toggles” with applications on our phone, is titled “The toggling leader” as he will explore a new concept in governance due to the ever changing circumstances in the modern world and its implication for our future leaders.
