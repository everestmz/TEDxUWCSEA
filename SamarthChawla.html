Speaker Profile

Samarth Chawla

My name is Samarth Chawla. I am a 17-year-old Indian living in Singapore. My Ted talk is on “rethinking consumerism”. It is inspired from my love for economics. In the talk, I discuss how and why we need to change what it means to be a consumer in the 21st century.
