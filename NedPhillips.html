Speaker Profile

Ned Phillips

Ned, age 47 who is originally from Scotland has been living in Asia since 1991. Having traveled extensively through Asia he has settled and lived in Singapore for the past 11 years, and previously lived in Hong Kong for 12 years. Ned has a wife and two kids and currently works in the finance industry. His talk will focus on another of his passions, endurance sports, and the use of the mind to enhance performance. Ned has taken part in a range of long distance events from the Ironman World Championships, to 100k running races, to marathons, to a 900k mountain bike race across Mongolia and for fun one day he ran round Singapore in one 18 hour period covering 125k. Throughout all this the one constant has been the use of the mind to control how events unfold. Ned will be speaking about the concept of the Now and how we can all perform better than any of us thinks.
