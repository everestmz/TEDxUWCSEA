Speaker Profile

Roshni Karwal

Roshni Karwal is a journalist, television presenter and executive producer of Sri-Lankan-Singaporean descent. She has worked with the BBC, CNBC, MTV and the United Nations, covering events such as September 11th from Ground Zero to the 2010 earthquake and tsunami in Japan. She has been trained in Climate Reality Leadership by former Vice-President Al Gore, a topic that she continues to pursue through her work. She has decided to share some of her expertise with us through her talk: Conquering Carbon, which is about the impacts of carbon pollution in the United States.
