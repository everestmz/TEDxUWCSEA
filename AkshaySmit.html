Speaker Profile

Akshay Smit

Akshay is a senior year student at UWCSEA with a deep fascination for maths and philosophy. His exploration of the combination of the two is the basis of his talk 'Limitations of Logic - or the Human Brain?' where he will guide us through some simple (and complicated) logical pathways to arrive at a conclusion we may not have expected.
